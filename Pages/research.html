Inspiration for Home Page

Inspiration Webpage

We were inspired by the home page of the Australian Bonsai Store, which features a clean and modern design, with a focus on showcasing their products. The home page features a simple layout that is easy to navigate, with a featured products section and a gallery of photos that showcase the beauty of bonsai trees. The store also offers a blog, where customers can learn more about the art of bonsai and find helpful tips.

Our Webpage

We took this inspiration and implemented it into our own website home page by creating a simple and modern design with a focus on showcasing our products. We also included a featured products section and a gallery of photos that we curated to showcase the beauty of our products. To further help our customers, we included a blog section where customers can find helpful tips, articles, and other resources to help them learn more about the products we offer. Additionally, we have also included social links, making it easy for customers to stay connected and up-to-date on the latest news and products.

Inspiration for product Page

Inspiration Webpage

Inspiration for making product page of our website is the Amazon Bonsai Store. The speciality of Amazon Bonsai Store product page is its detailed description of the product accompanied with a high quality image. The image of the product gives customer a crystal clear idea of how the product looks and what they can expect from the product.

Our Webpage

We have been inspired from Amazon Bonsai Store product page and implemented many of the same features in making our product page. We have added detailed description of the product with a high quality image. Along with that, we have also added an add to cart button which Amazon Bonsai Store does not have. This will make it easier for customers to add the product to their cart without having to click through different pages. This will also help us to increase sales and customer engagement.

Inspiration for Functional Form Page

Inspiration Webpage

Eastern Leaf is a home decor and gift shop that is known for its creative and unique products. Its order page is intuitive and user-friendly, with a sleek design and engaging visuals. The page features an easy-to-navigate grid layout, allowing customers to quickly find what they are looking for and add products to their cart. The page also displays additional product images and descriptions, helping customers make informed decisions about their purchases.

Our Webpage

We were inspired by Eastern Leaf’s order page when creating our own website’s order page. We implemented the grid layout and product images to help customers find what they need quickly and easily. However, we made a few changes to the design. We made the layout horizontal instead of the vertical layout used by Eastern Leaf. This allows customers to clearly see all of their options at once, making it easier to find what they’re looking for.
